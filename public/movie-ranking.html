Best Movies According to Tao

My top 3 movies of all-the-time

12 Angry Men

The jury in a New York City murder trial is frustrated by a single member whose skeptical caution
forces them to more carefully consider the evidence before jumping to a hasty verdict.

Apocalypse Now

A U.S. Army officer serving in Vietnam is tasked with assassinating a renegade Special Forces
Colonel who sees himself as a god.

The Deer Hunter

An in-depth examination of the ways in which the Vietnam War impacts and disrupts the lives of
several friends in a small steel mill town in Pennsylvania.
